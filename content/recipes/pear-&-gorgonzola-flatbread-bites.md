## Pear & Gorgonzola Flatbread Bites

**Ingredients:**
- 1 package of pre-made flatbread
- 2 ripe pears, thinly sliced
- 1 cup of crumbled Gorgonzola cheese
- 1/2 cup of chopped walnuts
- 2 tablespoons of honey
- Fresh arugula leaves (for garnish)
- Olive oil (for drizzling)

**Instructions:**
1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
2. Place the pre-made flatbread on a baking sheet or pizza stone.
3. Layer the thinly sliced pears evenly on top of the flatbread.
4. Sprinkle the crumbled Gorgonzola cheese over the pears.
5. Scatter the chopped walnuts on top of the cheese.
6. Drizzle the honey evenly over the flatbread.
7. Place the baking sheet or pizza stone in the preheated oven and bake for about 10-12 minutes, or until the cheese melts and the flatbread becomes crispy.
8. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
9. Cut the flatbread into bite-sized squares or triangles.
10. Transfer the bites to a serving platter or individual plates.
11. Garnish with fresh arugula leaves.
12. Drizzle with a small amount of olive oil for added flavor (optional).
13. Serve warm and enjoy these delectable Pear & Gorgonzola Flatbread Bites!

Tip: These bites make a great appetizer or snack for a party or gathering.
